About Color Space

Color Space shows HSV using a polar coordinate view:

  • Hue: the angle around the base disk (0–360°)
  • Saturation: the distance from the center (radius 0–1)
  • Brightness: the height up the vertical axis (0–1)

In simple terms: pick a direction for Hue, move outwards by Saturation, then go up by Brightness.

Under the hood this maps to the scene like so (from the code):

  • x = cos(Hue) × Saturation × radius
  • z = sin(Hue) × Saturation × radius
  • y = Brightness × height
